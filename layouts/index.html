About Me

I am a New York based UX designer and researcher with two years of experience designing for web and mobile. I hold a master’s in human-computer interaction from the University of Michigan and a bachelor’s in psychology from Binghamton University.

I take a human-centered approach to design, keeping both aesthetics and usability in mind to deliver meaningful, delightful experiences. I’ve worked on teams as both a designer and a programmer; this has given me a broad understanding and knowledge needed to find solutions that balance bold design with development costs.

When I am not working, my hobbies include sketching and drawing (mainly animals).

I am currently looking for a full time position in UX design. If you are you looking for a UX designer that champions the user, or just want to grab a coffee, send me an email at garycheu23@nullgmail.com!
