About Me

Hi, my name is Gary. I am a UX Designer and Web Developer that uses a human-centered approach to create delightful experiences.

I was initially a latecomer to the world of tech, having learnt to code right after finishing my undergraduate degree. During my time away from school, my background in psychology led to become curious about the human facing side of software development. This lead me to return to school where I learned to bring human-centered principles into design and development.

I am seeking fulltime opportunities in UX design. If you are looking for a designer that believes in the importance of UX, feel free to contact me at garycheu23@gmail.com!
